25 Tips for Writing an Outstanding Resume

Resume Writing Tips

  • Keep it concise.
  • Use a professional email address.
  • Include relevant keywords from the job description.
  • Use action verbs to describe your experiences.
  • Quantify your achievements.
  • Tailor your resume for each job application.
  • Highlight your most relevant skills.
  • Use a clean and simple layout.
  • Include a summary statement.
  • Focus on your accomplishments, not just duties.
  • Proofread multiple times.
  • Use bullet points for easy readability.
  • Include relevant coursework or projects for recent graduates.
  • Use a consistent font style and size.
  • Include volunteer work if applicable.
  • Avoid using personal pronouns.
  • Keep your resume to one or two pages.
  • Showcase your education and certifications.
  • Include links to professional profiles (e.g., LinkedIn).
  • Make sure your contact information is current.
  • Include industry-specific terminology.
  • Avoid jargon and overly complex language.
  • Use numbers and metrics to demonstrate impact.
  • Include a cover letter for a more personal touch.
  • Update your resume regularly.
